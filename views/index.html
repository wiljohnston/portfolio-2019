Pocket

to align with the 2019 accenture hackathon theme, banking revolution, we developed a banking app with a focus on UX. Pocket uses spend categorisation to let users create virtual bank accounts that are assigned to specific spend categories. Users can then apply spending limits to these vitual accounts.

it was a lot of fun building the app under huge time pressure (24 hours), and we had even more fun presenting it. We were able to win over the judges and take out first place - a delightfully exciting way to close a massive weekend.
